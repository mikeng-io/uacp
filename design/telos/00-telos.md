# The Telos (root)

> **DRAFT — for red-pen (#98).** Reframes `comprehend-measure-serialize/` onto the purpose it
> serves. This node stays thin — one entity per node — so substance lives in 10–50.

## Purpose (the end)
UACP exists to **decrease the friction of cooperation** on work done by *semantic*
(non-deterministic) actors — agents, and the humans who direct them — by making one thing
automatic and carried forward: **the binding of declared intent to witnessed evidence.**

Everything else — CMS, Heartgate, the gates and witnesses, the lifecycle, the memory
substrate — is **machinery in service of that end.** Coherence and auditability are *costs the
machinery pays*, never the end. Optimizing the means (coherence for its own sake) against the
end (less friction) is the failure this bundle exists to prevent.

## The mechanism, in one line
UACP is a **conformance loop**: it makes *"does the realized reality match the declared
intent?"* the governed atom, closed across the lifecycle and across runs, enforced against an
actor who cannot be trusted to hold the binding himself. It is **neither a spec framework nor a
test framework** — those are the frozen write-side and check-side snapshots of the one relation
it governs.

## Relationship to the CMS bundle
CMS (comprehend → *evaluate* → serialize) is the **coherence discipline chosen to serve this
purpose** — the loop instantiated at a single grain. **CMS derives from the telos, not the
reverse.** The CMS bundle already softened "axiom" → "coherence invariant / chosen discipline";
this bundle supplies the purpose that discipline was chosen *for*, and corrects the standing
inversion (#98) where CMS floats above the purpose it is supposed to serve. See **50** for the
reconciliation plan.

- **20 — recursive critique.** The third leg: the witness is also fallible, so no verdict is

- **40 — derivations + friction budget.** What derives from the telos (CMS, gates, lifecycle,
