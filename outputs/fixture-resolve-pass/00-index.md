# Fixture RESOLVE Closure Package

## Reading order
This package records closure semantics for RESOLVE. Read closure summary, final decision, residual risks, lessons and dispositions, state and memory disposition, and operator handoff. It explains what closed, what did not close, what risks remain, and what next actions are explicitly out of scope.

## Intent and evidence
The intent is final lifecycle closure. Evidence is the run-bound VERIFY readiness artifact plus closure selection and closure decision artifacts.
