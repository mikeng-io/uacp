# Council and Review Topology

## PLAN council
A focused PLAN council must review this plan before EXECUTE if time/flow permits. Minimum roles:
- Runtime / Enforcement Reviewer
- Compression Auditor

## VERIFY council
Mandatory after EXECUTE. Must inspect actual diffs, validator results, Heartgate dry-runs, and fixture behavior.

## Follow-through rule
Material findings must be classified as remediated, deferred with owner/risk, accepted warning, or blocker. Do not bury negative findings in prose.
